About Me

Hi, I’m Navya! I am a senior at Milton High School interested in materials science and polymer chemistry for sustainability. I aim to study materials engineering in college, designing polymers for biomaterials, fabric, and electronics. I am fascinated by the potential of novel materials to minimize our environmental footprint, and I aim to explore this space using synthetic chemistry and mathematical models.

Recently, I worked on a project where I developed a machine-learning based virtual screening method to filter and analyze biodegradable polyesters. Previously, I worked on computational modeling and biochemical analysis for the early diagnosis and treatment of various diseases. Check out my portfolio here!

I also enjoy organizing various human rights initiatives through Milton’s chapter of Amnesty International, and you can see our projects here! Some of my hobbies include reading fictional books (ex. A Thousand Splendid Suns, The Color Purple, Frankenstein), hiking (loved Zion and Olympiad national parks!), and playing the violin.

AI-based System to Predict High Risk Pregnancies

Independent Science Fair Project
